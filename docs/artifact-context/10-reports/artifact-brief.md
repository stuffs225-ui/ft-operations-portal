## Review Checklist

- [ ] Reports hub has clear categorization by module
- [ ] Date range selector is prominent on every report
- [ ] Chart and table views are switchable
- [ ] Export (PDF/Excel) CTA is visible
- [ ] Per-role report pages show only data relevant to that role

## Artifact Instructions

Reports are data-dense — redesign must prioritise readability over decoration.
Export CTAs must remain accessible in any redesign.
