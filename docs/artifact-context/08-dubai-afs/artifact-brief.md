## Review Checklist

- [ ] AFS dashboard shows open projects and delivery status at a glance
- [ ] PN gate shows blocked projects prominently
- [ ] Missing items list blocks pre-delivery until resolved
- [ ] ETA tracking shows history of changes with reasons
- [ ] Arrival reports are clearly distinct from pre-delivery reports
- [ ] Ready for delivery is a clear status (QC Release Note required)

## Artifact Instructions

PN gate is a required step — preserve its visual enforcement.
Missing items block readiness — surface this clearly in any redesign.
Dubai AFS is geographically separate from Saudi factory — maintain that distinction.
